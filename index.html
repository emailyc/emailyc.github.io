Background

This presentation is a reproducible pitch presentation for the Shiny app project for the Developing Data Product course. This presentations is created using Slidify.

Purpose

The app displays a distribution plot of different statistical distributions. The user may choose from a list of distribution and the specify the sample size. The plot will then update itself according to the parameters of the user. The plot is best used by those who wish to have a better understanding of statistical distributions and their relationships with sample sizes.

How to use the app

On the left, there are two toggles:

  1. A drop down list
  2. A slide bar

The user may choose a list of statistical distribution on the drop down list;
and the sample size using the slide bar.

Finally, click the submit button to refresh the plot
